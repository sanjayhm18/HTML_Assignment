VIJAYASHREE PUBLIC SCHOOL - HOW TO APPLY FOR ADMISSIONS

The admission process in Vijayashree Public School is very simple. The admission registration begins in the month of September of the previous calendar year. Admissions are granted on a first-come-first-served basis for available seats.

The following are the steps for the admission process:

STEP 1: MAKE AN ENQUIRY

You can make an admission enquiry through – Filling out the form


Call us on: +91-96633 55775
Email us at: enquiry@vijayashreepublicschool.com
Once we have received the enquiry, the concerned personnel will get in touch with you

STEP 2: VISIT OUR SCHOOL

If you are based in Bangalore, you can make an appointment for a tour of the school infrastructure and child-friendly facilities that we have developed for our children.
In case you are currently not in Bangalore, we can provide any further information through a call or online interaction

STEP 3: APPLY FOR ADMISSION

If you believe that Vijayashree Public School is the right fit for your child, you would need to submit the Application Form and other relevant forms and documents, which will be shared with you by our admission team personnel after receiving your enquiry. For your information, the following forms and documents are required: One photograph of your child
· A copy of your child’s Birth Certificate
· Your child’s academic records / transcripts for the last 3 years (if applicable)
· If your child is not an Indian citizen, a copy of their visa.

STEP 4: ADMISSION INTERACTION

Once we receive all the required documents from your end and based on the grade your child is applying to, our admission personnel will schedule meetings with the concerned faculties or team members and arrange for placement tests to help us understand your child’s grade readiness.
If you have any questions about our Admission Process, please email us at: enquiry@vijayashreepublicschool.com
